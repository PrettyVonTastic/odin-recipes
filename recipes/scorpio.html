Scorpio: The Negroni

To represent the scorpion of the zodiac, we're going for a drink whose flavor profile is as complex as their personality. Be warned, however, this drink has a sting to it and is not for the timid.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1 ounce Campari
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  • Ice
  • Garnish: orange peel or slice
Steps
  1. Add the gin, Campari and sweet vermouth to a mixing glass filled with ice, and stir until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a rocks glass filled with large cubes.
  3. Garnish with orange.
