Leo: Blue Lagoon

A tiki bar favorite, the Blue Lagoon has refreshing notes of citrus mixed with sweet. The blue color certainly demands attention, much like the lion of the zodiac.


Ingredients
  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 1 ounce blue curaçao
  • 4 ounces lemonade
  • Garnish: lemon wheel
  • Garnish: maraschino cherry
Steps
  1. Add the vodka, blue curaçao, and lemonade to a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a hurricane glass over crushed ice.
  3. Garnish with a lemon wheel and maraschino cherry.
